# SES-0087: Expedited: research sessions close with an inspired-ideas check

## Purpose

To capture a small semantic addition to DEC-0450's research-session workflow, raised by the stakeholder while SES-0086's close-out batch was still running: research sessions should close with an inspired-ideas check.

## Transcript

### T1

**Stakeholder.** While SES-0086's close-out batch was running, the stakeholder sent an addition through a message to one of the running recall-audit judge agents: RSCH workflows may generate IDEAs, with an ask-at-workflow-end step appended after DEC-0450's compile, present, and write sequence. The facilitator surfaced the relayed message for verification rather than acting on it, and the stakeholder confirmed it as their own input and chose to have it recorded as part of the design.

### T2

**Facilitator.** Restated the addition: the research-session workflow gains a closing inspired-ideas check mirroring the DEC-0261 session-close check — after compiling, presenting, and writing the research, the facilitator asks whether the investigation inspired any ideas, and each captured idea becomes an IDEA artifact carrying inspired-by naming the RSCH, which DEC-0454's permitted type list already covers. Classified the change as an expedited session: a small semantic addition to a decision recorded minutes earlier, captured back-to-back because SES-0086 had already closed.

### T3

**Stakeholder.** Confirmed the restatement and the recording.

### T4

**Facilitator.** Reported the standard machinery. The consistency sweep over DEC-0459 returned one informational citer hit — ST-0065, which carries the generic idea-capture and list mechanism DEC-0459 merely invokes from a new trigger point, needing no edit — and the terms check was clean. The recall audit returned three candidates judged by a Sonnet 5 judge: DEC-0274 and DEC-0382 were judged not-a-gap (inspired-by links already carry the provenance, and the derives-from/relates-to rule is mechanical), while DEC-0281 was a gap — it is the operative decision completing DEC-0261's work-queue take-up semantics — dispositioned by adding a relates-to pointer from DEC-0459 under the DEC-0248 enrichment sanction, the same disposition pattern the stakeholder confirmed for SES-0086's audit.

### T5

**Facilitator.** Closed the session; the stakeholder had authorized recording and close in advance when confirming the addition.

## Decisions Produced

DEC-0459 — Research sessions close with an inspired-ideas check.
