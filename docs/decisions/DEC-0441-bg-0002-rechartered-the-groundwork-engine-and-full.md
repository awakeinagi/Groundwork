# DEC-0441: BG-0002 rechartered: the Groundwork Engine and full paradigm delivery as the core technology

## Context

SES-0085 took up IDEA-0058: the stakeholder observed that the Groundwork Application is mostly a presentation layer around the Groundwork Engine, and that BG-0002 — chartered as the method track's governance goal (DEC-0338, narrowed by DEC-0346, DEC-0421, and DEC-0422) — might be better suited to track development of the Engine itself as the core technology (SES-0085 T1). Grilling round 1 posed the charter-object question directly: what should rechartered BG-0002's object of intent be?

## Decision

BG-0002's object of intent is the development and refinement of the Groundwork Engine as the core technology, together with full delivery of the paradigm around it: skill-mode delivery, project adoption, runtime portability, third-party export, and self-governance. This supersedes DEC-0338's method-track charter framing.

## Rationale

The narrowings DEC-0346, DEC-0421, and DEC-0422 applied to DEC-0338 carry forward unchanged into the new charter; nothing they settled is reopened, and DEC-0423's single-sourcing and library-extraction trigger become charter-level constraints.

## Alternatives Considered

Round 1 offered three options for the charter object: Engine mechanics only; Engine plus its delivery surfaces (the facilitator's recommendation); and Engine plus full paradigm delivery. The stakeholder chose the third, broadest option — "Option 3. Plus I want topic specific epics..." (SES-0085 T7) — rejecting the narrower two.

## Implications

The Application itself — its UI, team-facing features, hosting, and native reimplementations — remains BG-0001's object, and BG-0001's own reframing toward a presentation-and-team layer on the Engine stays a separate future step, not bundled into this recharter.
