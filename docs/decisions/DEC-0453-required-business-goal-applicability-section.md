# DEC-0453: Required Business-Goal applicability section

## Context

Grilling round 3 (T9) asked whether the Business-Goal applicability analysis is required; the stakeholder (T10) confirmed it as required at conclusion.

## Decision

Every Research artifact must close each round with an applicability analysis mapping findings to existing Business Goals by bare ID and/or sketching candidate new Business Goals. "No applicability found" is an explicit valid outcome. This section is what the stakeholder reviews at the concluded transition.

## Rationale

The stakeholder's original proposal centered on connecting research to business intent; making the applicability section mandatory (rather than optional narrative) keeps that connection explicit and gives the ungated type's one review checkpoint a concrete artifact to review.

## Alternatives Considered

Making the applicability analysis optional was rejected — it is the section that carries the type's core purpose and the findings-review gate's substance.

## Implications

Each reopened round (DEC-0448) needs its own applicability section, since findings and their business relevance can change between rounds.
