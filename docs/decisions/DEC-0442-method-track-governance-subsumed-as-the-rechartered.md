# DEC-0442: Method-track governance subsumed as the rechartered BG-0002's self-governance outcome

## Context

The recharter required resolving what happens to BG-0002's existing governance-track charter (DEC-0338's five outcomes: gated contracts, no ungoverned capability, human gates before deployment, nothing ratified invisibly unbuilt, no build without intake). Round 1 offered three readings: subsume it as a self-governance outcome (recommended), split it into a separate governance Business Goal, or demote it to constraints only.

## Decision

The method-track governance charter is subsumed into the rechartered BG-0002 as its self-governance outcome: every deployed execution surface of the Engine and paradigm is documented, contracted, and gated by the same pipeline it implements.

## Rationale

The prior charter's five outcomes — gated contracts citing decisions; no ungoverned capability, mechanically checked; human gates before deployment; nothing ratified invisibly unbuilt; no build without the intake path — are condensed into that single outcome with their underlying decisions (DEC-0335, DEC-0340, DEC-0341, DEC-0344, DEC-0345) cited unchanged. A focused self-governance epic is anticipated under the rechartered goal.

## Alternatives Considered

The stakeholder rejected a standalone governance Business Goal, answering "This could be part of the goals and maybe deserves its own focused Epic" (SES-0085 T7) — choosing subsumption plus a focused epic over the split-BG and constraints-only options round 1 offered.

## Implications

No separate governance Business Goal is created. IDEA-0057, the operating-mode question for this repository once the Application exists, stays queued and is unaffected.
