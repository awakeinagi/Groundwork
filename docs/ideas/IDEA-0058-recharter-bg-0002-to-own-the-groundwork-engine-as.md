# IDEA-0058: Recharter BG-0002 to own the Groundwork Engine as the core technology

## The Idea

Captured verbatim at SES-0082 T51: "This dogfooding exercise has been very helpful in refining the trajectory for Groundwork. Looking back, I feel like I jumped the gun a bit in deciding for a full application from the start. I'm really seeing the benefit of the agents and skills working with methodology. The application itself really is mostly a presentation layer around the Groundwork Engine. Toward that realization, I'm starting to think that BG-0002 may be better suited to track the development and refinement of the Groundwork Engine itself as the core technology to work on, even if an application with a UI may have its own merits. Since BG-0002 is still relatively new, I think it might not be too hard to change its focus. What are your thoughts?"

## Spark Context

Raised at SES-0082's final close-out turns (T51), after the session's own delineation work: the Application is mostly a presentation layer around the Groundwork Engine (DEC-0423), skill-mode is the paradigm's core subset (DEC-0433), and BG-0002's charter has already evolved from sacrificial method-tooling governance (DEC-0338) through pattern permanence (DEC-0346) to permanent skill-mode delivery (DEC-0421, DEC-0422). The proposal is to refocus BG-0002 to track development and refinement of the Groundwork Engine itself as the core technology, with the application's UI merits acknowledged but repositioned rather than discarded.

Open questions for the take-up session: the fate of BG-0002's current governance-track charter (subsume as self-governance versus split out separately); the DEC-0267 cascade over BG-0002's derived tree, including EP-0009, the SP-0013 through SP-0018 spikes, and the many decisions citing BG-0002; whether EP-0009 and the Engine-related deferred work (the DEC-0423 library-extraction trigger, the DEC-0430 and DEC-0437 ID-allocation mechanics, and SP-0018) re-parent cleanly under a rechartered BG-0002; and the downstream implication for BG-0001's own framing toward a presentation-and-team layer on the Engine, noted as a further, separate step not to be bundled into the same take-up session.

## Disposition

Entered take-up on 2026-07-12 at SES-0085; completed the same day. BG-0002 rechartered as Groundwork Core Technology — Engine & Delivery of the Paradigm (DEC-0441 superseding DEC-0338; DEC-0442 governance subsumption; DEC-0443 roster plus its extension (DEC-0446); DEC-0444 skill-mode; DEC-0445 EP-0009 broadening). Cascade completed in-session: EP-0009 and SP-0013 through SP-0017 re-affirmed. BG-0001's reframing remains a separate future step.
