# IDEA-0065: BG-lineage filter for recall-audit retrieval

## The Idea

Give recall-audit retrieval a business-goal lineage scope tag or filter — or, short of filtering outright, judge-packet guidance flagging each candidate's BG lineage — so candidates from a superseded track or a sibling business goal are pre-marked rather than left for judges to discover by full read.

## Spark Context

SES-0089's recall audit surfaced many BG-0001 Application-track decisions as candidates against BG-0002 Engine epics purely on generic keyword overlap (git/gate/index/orchestration vocabulary); judges spent most of their effort rejecting cross-goal noise rather than evaluating genuine candidates. Raised as a judge finding during SES-0089's decision-recall audit (T19) and ratified for capture at T20.
