## The Idea

DEC-0472 through DEC-0474 assumed option descriptions render alongside
previews. Controlled experiments (2026-07-13) show preview layout
suppresses descriptions and the automatic Other option, single-question
preview cards force a choice between selecting an option and writing a
note, and the options column is hard-fixed near thirty characters. The
method guidance should shift to full analysis in chat before each
card, lean labels, previews reserved as small examples, and
two-plus-question cards when note-plus-selection matters.

## Spark Context

Discovered mid-grilling in SES-0092 when the stakeholder saw only
labels and truncated previews on a detailed card.
